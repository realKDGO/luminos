Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

Luminos ("we", "our", or "the store") is a premium tech accessories shop designed to elevate your workspace. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your information.

This policy explains what information Luminos collects when you visit our store, create an account, or place an order — and how that information is stored and protected. Please read it carefully before using the site.

Data We Collect

Luminos stores the information you enter directly into the site when you create an account and interact with the store:

  • Your display name, email address, and other account details you choose to save
  • Your password and login-related information as stored by the app on your device
  • Your cart contents, order history, saved items, and checkout details
  • Your display preferences such as theme and layout settings

How We Store Your Data

Luminos stores account, cart, order, and preference data locally in your browser using localStorage. The information stays on the device and browser profile where it was created unless you clear it manually or your browser removes it.

This means your data is:

  • Available only on the browser and device where it was saved
  • Not automatically synced across devices or browsers
  • Retained until you clear site data, reset your browser storage, or remove it through the app
  • Dependent on your device security and browser settings

If you clear your browser storage, some or all saved information may be lost.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to power the features of Luminos. Specifically:

  • To let you sign in on the same browser where your account data is stored
  • To save and display your cart, saved items, order history, and preferences
  • To keep the store functional between page loads and future visits on the same device
  • To support checkout and profile features inside the app

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Local Storage Notice

Luminos is designed to run primarily with data stored in your browser through localStorage. Account information, cart contents, order records, and interface preferences may remain saved until you remove them.

Because this storage is local to your device, anyone with access to the same browser profile may be able to view that information.

Your Rights

You have the following options regarding data stored by Luminos on your device:

  • Access — You can view stored profile, cart, and order information inside the app
  • Update — You can edit saved information through the relevant account or checkout screens
  • Delete — You can remove your stored data by clearing the app data or browser localStorage for this site

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Data is kept locally in your browser instead of being sent to a separate application database
  • Access to stored information depends on the security of your own device, browser profile, and account usage
  • You should avoid using shared or public devices when saving personal information in the app
  • Clearing localStorage or using private browsing may remove or limit saved data

No browser-based storage method is completely risk-free, so you should use appropriate caution when entering personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the change. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Continued use of Luminos after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions or concerns? This is a personal project — feel free to reach out via the store's feedback channels.
